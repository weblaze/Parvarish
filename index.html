Introducing
PARVARISH

Our Lakshya

The concept of daycares is very prevalent in the west and not so much in our country, but now when we are moving from joint families to nuclear families and both the parents are working we feel the need too. We at PARVARISH aim to aid you in that, want to make the parents aware about the advantages of daycares and how their children can still be taken care of while they themselves are at the office trying to give their children the best life they can. It is true that nothing can replace the love of parents but we assure you that we will provide you the second best thing to it and make sure that you don't have to worry about their safety and convenience.


OUR PARICHAY

According to a recent survey 62.3% families in India have both parents working, of these parents are in need of a decent day-care facility and have to tour the entire town comparing various day-cares which leads to unwanted stress and wastage of resources. We at PARVARISH aim to solve that problem by providing parents a platform to view, compare and book day-care facilities at one place that too at costs less than market price.


WHY PARVARISH

We are a service like no other. The option to view, compare and book day care facilities at one place is very comfortable for any working parents and comes off as THE perfect solution. The great personal customer service which we will provide to the customers would be a major source of attraction. The experience of the founders in the respective fields along with the zeal to do well benefits the company as well. As of yet, there is no other website or app that is doing things how we plan to do. Our motto 'A Second Home' very well defines what we want to do with the company and what kind of experience we want to provide to the customers.





Khaasiyat

One-Stop Solution

Remember surfing the internet for countless hours, calling every day-care or touring around the entire city just to find the perfect facility for your child and even after all this not being completely sure if your children will be treated like those adorable little souls deserve to be? Well, that's NOT a problem anymore, PARVARISH is a one-stop solution for viewing, comparing, and booking day-cares at the same place without the hassle of calls or waiting for replies, or taking leaves from your office to look for one. Now you can focus on your work without worrying about your children being mistreated or mishandled.

Transparency

We at PARVARISH completely understand your love for your children and you being protective of them.We ensure that we give you each and every detail about the day-care facility ranging from security checks to food to CCTV cameras and even the backgrounds of the employees when it comes to our PARVARISH-approved daycares. We want you to have all the information you need to make a well-informed decision for your child and have a sense of security and satisfaction when you drop them off at the daycare every morning.

Parent Angel Program

We know how much trust you put in us when you send your children to daycares and we don't plan to break that trust. Every once in a while we conduct checks at every facility of ours and we thought who would know better about what is better for the children other than their parents.


We wish to make the parents OUR GUARDIAN ANGELS. We aim to adhere to a system wherein we ensure quality checks at ground zero with the help of our parents because whats better than feedback from someone who will always keep their childs wellbeing their topmost priority. We would love to provide the parents that volunteer with discounts and coupon codes on their next purchase and make this dream come true, TOGETHER.
